Winning Movies:
What makes an Oscar? More than 1000 movies give insights

Is it the buget, the director, or the actors? What other vairables are involved with creating an oscar winning film? In the journy to answering these questions we (The Data Knights) found two different data sets from Kaggle.com. One of which contained movie information for over 1,000 movies, the other dataset had Oscar winning information. We merged these dataset, processed, split and trained a Machine Learning model to first find which variables were most importaint, and second be able to predict if a combination of Director, Actor and Budget would produce a Winning Oscar Award (or maybe a nomination). We also thought it would be fun if you could search for your favorite movie in the Winners tab. The Money tab tells the story through budget and profit. With so many questions we answered a few of them in our Visualize tab. You can find our summary in the Results tab.
